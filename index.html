AI Digital Nurse Twin

A virtual caregiver that never sleeps — a living model of each resident that continuously learns their health and behaviour to deliver personalised, predictive care.

Bathroom Safety

The bathroom is one of the highest-risk places in care. AusAge360 monitors visits and patterns to detect prolonged time, unusual routines or missed visits — and intervene earlier.

Sleep & Rest Intelligence

Quality sleep drives health, mood and recovery. We monitor sleep patterns and night-time disruption to help teams support better rest, calmer days and happier residents.

Actionable Insights

Intuitive dashboards and smart alerts bring real-time intelligence, task management and outcome tracking together — delivering the right insight to the right person at the right time.

How it works

Sense. Predict. Protect.

Three layers working together — from ambient signal to confident, proactive action.

  1. 01

    Ambient intelligence

    Advanced, non-intrusive sensors — 60GHz radar, environmental and acoustic — continuously capture movement and wellbeing data. No cameras. Ever.

  2. 02

    AI & analytics engine

    AI models analyse patterns in real time to detect change, predict risk and surface anomalies through each resident's Digital Nurse Twin.

  3. 03

    Intelligent action

    The right insight reaches the right person at the right moment — smart alerts, care-team dashboards and workflow integration that turn data into care.
